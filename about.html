ABOUT US

Agency Grimm is a Catholic supernatural detective story set against the vibrant backdrop of the Jazz Age. Join the charismatic Matilda, dour Jacob, and silly Milly as they take on supernatural baddies and get back in time for lunch.

At its heart, the series is the story of the Grimm siblings, who run a detective agency specializing in cases that touch the supernatural. They are bound together by the precedent set out by their ancestors a hundred years before: to document the strange, the miraculous, and the uncanny, and help prevent history from repeating itself.

The world they investigate is one where European folklore is a tangible reality. Hobgoblins haunt the docks and witches plot in penthouse apartments. But for the Grimms, the most powerful forces are the stories we all weave and the truths we tell ourselves—and what happens when we finally come "face to face".

An urban fantasy exploring themes of family, forgiveness, and flawed characters. It aims to be a gripping urban fantasy for readers who enjoy smart world-building and character-driven stories

Explore the agency’s archives, listen to their case files, and discover a world where every shadow holds a story, and the greatest secrets lie in the depths of the sealed family vault.
