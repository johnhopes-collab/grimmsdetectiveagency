The Case Files

Agency Grimm is a Catholic urban fantasy series set against the vibrant backdrop of 1920s New York. In this world, goblins wear zoot suits and chomp on stogies, pixies sue the mayor, and witches plot in penthouse apartments. Prohibition is in full swing and the Big Bad Wolf is a bootlegger. Join the charismatic Matilda, dour Jacob, and silly Milly as they take on supernatural bad guys and make it back in time for breakfast.

The Grimm siblings are bound together by the precedent set by the original Brothers Grimm a hundred years ago: to solve the strange, to document the miraculous, to banish the monstrous. For the Grimms, the most powerful forces aren't witches or warlocks, but the power of faith and family. The Case Files document their journey in the second half of the 1920s, a series that will appeal to fans of urban fantasy, philology nerds, and the contemporary Catholic experience.

10% of proceeds go to Catholic charities (Magnificat House Homeless Shelters and FSSP).


About the Author

J.C. Hopes is a mysterious, reclusive man who doubles as Houston's most popular karaoke singer. He can be found either stalking the streets of the city at midnight or reading Ray Bradbury on his front porch, and has made a very specific request for Russian vodka for his birthday. He is not a New York Times Bestseller, he does not live in Montana, and he does not have a wife with kids or two dogs.
