About the Book Series

Agency Grimm is a Catholic urban fantasy set against the vibrant backdrop of the 1920s New York. Join the charismatic Matilda, dour Jacob, and silly Milly as they take on supernatural baddies and get back in time for lunch. They are bound together by the precedent set by their ancestors a hundred years ago: to document the strange, to solve the miraculous, and to banish the uncanny. Goblins chomp on stoagies in zoot suits, witches plot in penthouse apartments, and Prohibition is in full swing, but for the Grimms, the most powerful forces are the stories we all tell ourselves—and what happens when we must finally come "face to face".

Please enjoy,
