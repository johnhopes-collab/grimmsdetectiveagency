Aarne-Thompson Index of Fairy Tale Types

The Aarne-Thompson Index is a scholarly catalogue of folktale types, developed by Finnish folklorist Antti Aarne and later expanded by Stith Thompson. It classifies traditional tales according to recurring plots, motifs, and narrative patterns found across cultures. Hover over any tale name to view its full description.
